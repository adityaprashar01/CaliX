# Pricing

Calix is built for busy parents of screen-heavy kids who avoid sports and need a safe, structured 5-minute movement habit at home.

## Plans

### Free
For families who want to try the core habit loop.

**Includes:**
- Limited beginner Quick Quests
- Basic avatar setup
- Basic streaks and rewards
- One child profile
- Limited parent progress view

**Price:** ₹0

---

### Calix Plus

## Why this pricing exists

Calix is not trying to replace sports academies or coaching centers.

It solves a smaller but more frequent problem:
**helping a child complete one safe, enjoyable 5-minute movement session today — and come back tomorrow.**

Parents pay for:
- trust
- structure
- visible progress
- lower friction than offline classes

Schools pay for:
- simple adoption
- group participation
- reporting
- habit-building at scale

## Trial philosophy

The product should prove value before asking for commitment.

That is why Calix uses a **14-day Visible Growth Trial**:
- kids experience fun and progress
- parents see consistency and safety
- conversion happens after visible value, not before
